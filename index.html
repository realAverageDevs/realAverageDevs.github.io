Deploying Diver Data to Your Server

  1. 1Get the Bot Invite Link: Click here to invite Diver Data to your server.
  2. 2Select Your Server: Choose the server where you want to add the bot. You must have Administrator or Manage Server permissions.
  3. 3Grant the Required Permissions: The bot needs the following:
    • ✅ Read Messages
    • ✅ Send Messages
    • ✅ Embed Links
    • ✅ Use Slash Commands
  4. 4Complete the CAPTCHA: Verify you’re human to finish adding the bot.
  5. 5Test the Bot: Type /help or another command in a channel to confirm it's working.
  6. 6(Optional) Adjust Bot Permissions: Ensure the bot has the correct permissions in the channel settings.
  7. 7Join the Support Server: If you have issues, join our Support Discord.
